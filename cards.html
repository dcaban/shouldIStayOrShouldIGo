FEMA recommends one gallon per person per day.
  • Water is not just used for drinking but can be used for bathing, cleaning, and flushing toilets if needed.

  • Consider filling the bathtub and any container you have with water before the storm.
  • Consider not just stocking up water but, items such as the life straw and iodine tablets to purify water if there is contamination.

    NON-PERISHABLE FOOD

    • Tips
    • How Much
    • Buy
    As much as your budget will let you afford -- you can always eat it later.
  • Healthy food with lots of protein should take priority over junk-food.

  • Consider the sodium content of the food. The more sodium you have the more water supply you will go through.
  • Many stores will have sales of fruits and vegetables the day before the hurricane. Most fruits and vegetables do not need refrigeration.
  • Try to eat everything in the fridge first in case of a power outage.
